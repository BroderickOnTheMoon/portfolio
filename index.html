DESIGN PROJECTS

CRAFT FOR CHANGE

Craft For Change

Thesis Project

SketchBook | Adobe Photoshop | Adobe Illustrator

A series of 3 craft beer cans. Packaging features digitally painted artwork of 3 different endangered animals. Each can represents it’s respective animal and half the profits made go towards a charity to help that animal. Included on the packaging is a QR code leading the customer to a website with animated art of the paintings. There they will also be encouraged to donate directly or purchase more of the product. Each can has a different approach to it’s colour and artwork while always making the animal its center focus.

DREAM ARCADES

Dream Arcades Logo

Dream Arcades

Adobe Photoshop | Adobe Illustrator

A logo redesign for the company Dream Arcades. The new logo has a vibrant, neon-chrome look to it and contains a vector drawing of an arcade machine. While keeping in mind that Dream Arcades sells vintage arcade machines, the vibrant chrome look was chosen as the main branding to reflect the style popularized in the 80’s. The vector art can also double as a smaller version of the company logo, allowing some versatility in how it is displayed. This allows the company to use a smaller variation of the logo when needed, such as a social media profile picture.

POSTER

Film Festival Poster

Ideas & Images 2

Adobe Photoshop | Adobe Illustrator

A poster designed to promote a film festival taking place in Japan. This poster combines and blends the imagery of movies and a Japaneses aesthetic. The large red film reel in the middle of the poster is not only eye catching, but is visually similar to the red circle in the flag of Japan. This poster demonstrates the idea of paring an event with a location and blending them together to create an aesthetically pleasing and creative poster.

Niagara Nutrition Partners Hand Out

Niagara Nutrition Partners Hand Out

A handout to promote Niagara Nutrition Partners. This handout contains an updated company logo, an easy to read description of what they do and a QR code leading to their website. About

Broderick Alderdice is a local Graphic Designer and Artist from the Niagara Region. Specializing in digital painting, he is always seeking for the balance between design and art. He loves to challenge and explore current design trends while expanding his catalog. He is inspired and influenced by pop culture, film, music, skate culture and most importantly, his peers!
